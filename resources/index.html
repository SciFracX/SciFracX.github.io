Resources about learning fractional order computing and modeling

Referrence

These resources are also the referrence for the numerical implementation of packages in SciFracX, some numerical algorithms are taken from these books.

There are still lots of marvelous books about fractional order computing we are going to explore~

Books​

  • Fractional Differential Equations by Professor Igor Podlubny

  • Fractional Calculus: Models and Numerical Methods by Professor Dumitru Baleanu, Kai Diethelm, Enrico Scalas and Juan J. Trujillo

  • Fractional-Order Control Systems: Fundamentals and Numerical Implementations by Professor Dingyü Xue

  • Numerical Methods for Fractional Calculus by Professor Changpin Li and Fanhai Zeng

  • 分数阶Fourier变换的原理与应用 陶然, 齐林, 王越著

  • Fractional-order Modeling and Control of Dynamic Systems by Professor Aleksei Tepljakov

  • Distributed-Order Dynamic Systems: Stability, Simulation, Applications and Perspectives by Professor Zhuang Jiao, YangQuan Chen, Igor Podlubny

  • Applications of Fractional Calculus in Physics by Professor R. Hilfer
