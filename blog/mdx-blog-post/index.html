Hello World!

January 7, 2022 ¬∑ One min read
Qingyu Qu
SciFracX maintainer

Hello all!

The official website of SciFracX project is now published using Docusaurus!

SciFracX is an orgnization aiming at making fractional order computing and modeling easier than ever with Julia.

Now, there are four packages under SciFracX, FractionalSystems.jl, FractionalDiffEq.jl, FractionalCalculus.jl and FractionalTransforms.jl.

If you are also interested in fractional order computing and modeling, JuliaLang and numerical methods binding math and programming, welcome to join SciFracX!!!!!
